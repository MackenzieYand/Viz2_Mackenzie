Participation in Soccer by the Numbers

As is to be expected we can see a noticeable drop off in nearly all the main streams of soccer due to the effects of the covid pandemic shown by the white dot. Except both Social and Futsal have grown, this may suggest a growing support of the game outside of more serious competetion.

Participation by Gender

Soccer has always been a male dominated sport but with the increasing popularity of the Matildas (big up Sam Kerr) the sport has grown in popularity among women.

Participation By State

Continued Growth

The noticeable setback of covid in 2020, all states other than New South Wales have continued to grow beyond their pre-covid numbers.

A-League Attendance

The growth seen in participation is interestingly contrasted by a decrease in support for the A-league Australias soccer league, with many teams seeing a lower turnout year on year.
